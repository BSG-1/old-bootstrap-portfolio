Crystal Collector

How to play the game


  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game that you are to match.
  2. You will do this via the 4 cyrstals below.
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Red
    • Yellow
  3. Clicking on a crystal will add a specific amount of points to your total. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click it.
  4. You win the game by matching your total to the random number.
  5. You lose the game if your total goes over the random number.
  6. Each time the game begins, the random number along with the values of each crystal, will change.
